Are you working in the Life Sciences with data? Do you feel overwhelmed when you think about Research Data Management?

The ELIXIR Research Data Management Kit (RDMKit) is an online guide containing good data management practices applicable to research projects from the beginning to the end. Developed and managed by people who work every day with life science data, RDMKit has guidelines, information and pointers, organised in many different ways to help you with problems throughout the dataâ€™s life cycle.
